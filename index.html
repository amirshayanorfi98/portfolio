About

Directing machines,
frame by frame.

I'm Amirshayan Orfi — a generative AI artist and video specialist based in London. I direct generative models the way a filmmaker directs a crew: deliberate lighting, composition, camera movement and pacing, so every sequence reads as authored cinema rather than model output. A background in architecture — BSc Architecture, MA Interior Design, and an RICS-accredited MSc — trained the eye that now builds these worlds.
